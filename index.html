Gal Gadot

The Modern Day Wonder Woman

“If things had gone according to my plans, I'd be a lawyer. I never dreamt of being an actress.”

Miss Israel 2008. Israeli Soldier. Model. Actress. Wife. Mother. Wonder Woman. Gal Gadot has nealy done it all. Gal Gadot is an Israeli actress best know for her role as Wonder Woman in the epynomous movie in the DC Extended Universe.

Gal-Gadot Varsano was born April 30th, 1985 in Israel in the city of Petah Tikva.

She grew up with a younger sister named Dana and her parents Irit and Michael Gadot. Gal grew up in "a very Jewish, very Israeli household" where her parents stressed the importance of family to Gal and her sister.

In high school, she majored in biology and was a star player on her school's basketball team. She eventually went on to study law in college as well.

After serving two year is in Israeli military, winning Miss Israel 2008, and getting married and having two daughters with her husband, Yaron Varsano, Gal had her biggest acting role to date as Wonder Woman in the summer of 2017. The film received critcal acclaim and beacuse of the success of the first Wonder Woman, a sequel is slated for November of 2019.
